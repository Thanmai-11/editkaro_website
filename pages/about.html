How We Got Here.

EditKaro.in was founded in 2022 by two college friends who had spent weekends making football edits and AMVs for fun. When those edits started getting thousands of views, brands came knocking — and a business was born.

What started as a bedroom operation with a laptop and a cracked copy of Premiere Pro is now a full-service creative studio with a team of specialist editors covering every content vertical.

We've edited product launches that broke ₹10 crore in revenue in their first week, gaming montages with 5M+ views, and wedding films that have made people cry on three continents. The common thread: every project gets our full attention, regardless of budget or brand size.
